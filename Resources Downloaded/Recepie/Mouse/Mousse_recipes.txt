Classic Chocolate Mousse
Ingredients:
- 200g dark chocolate
- 3 eggs, separated
- 50g sugar
- 300ml heavy cream
Instructions:
1. Melt chocolate and let cool slightly.
2. Whisk egg yolks into melted chocolate.
3. Beat egg whites with sugar until stiff peaks form.
4. Whip cream until soft peaks form.
5. Fold egg whites and whipped cream into chocolate mixture.
6. Chill for 2-3 hours before serving.

White Chocolate Mousse
Ingredients:
- 200g white chocolate
- 2 eggs, separated
- 250ml heavy cream
Instructions:
1. Melt white chocolate and cool slightly.
2. Whisk in egg yolks.
3. Whip cream until soft peaks form.
4. Fold whipped cream and beaten egg whites into chocolate.
5. Chill for 2 hours.

Lemon Mousse
Ingredients:
- 150ml lemon juice
- 150g sugar
- 3 eggs
- 250ml heavy cream
Instructions:
1. Whisk eggs and sugar over gentle heat until thick.
2. Add lemon juice and cool.
3. Whip cream and fold into lemon mixture.
4. Chill for 2 hours.

- 2 tbsp instant coffee
- 200g dark chocolate
- 3 eggs
- 250ml heavy cream
Instructions:
