Name of the Tart: The Best Lemon Tart Ever
Ingredients:
- Crust: ¾ cup butter, ½ cup white sugar, ½ tsp vanilla extract, pinch salt, 1¾ cups all-purpose flour
- Filling: 1½ cups white sugar, 3 large eggs, ½ cup lemon juice, ½ cup flour, 1 tbsp lemon zest
Instructions:
Make crust by mixing butter, sugar, vanilla, and salt; add flour to form dough. Press into tart pan and chill. Bake at 350°F for 15–20 min. Whisk filling ingredients, pour into crust, bake 20 min until set. Cool and dust with sugar.

Name of the Tart: Sweet and Simple Lemon Tart
Ingredients:
- Crust: ½ cup butter, ¼ cup confectioners’ sugar, 1 cup flour
- Filling: 1 cup sugar, 2 eggs, 3 tbsp flour, 3 tbsp lemon juice, 2 tsp zest, ¼ tsp salt
Instructions:
Preheat oven to 350°F. Mix crust ingredients, press into pan, bake 10–15 min. Whisk filling, pour into crust, bake 20–25 min until set. Cool and dust with sugar.

Name of the Tart: Traditional Bakewell Tart
Ingredients:
- Shortcrust pastry, 7 tbsp flour, 3 tbsp ground almonds, ¼ tsp baking powder, 4 tbsp raspberry jam, 1 egg, ¼ tsp lemon zest, ¼ cup butter, ¼ cup sugar, sliced almonds
Instructions:
Preheat oven to 375°F. Line pastry in tin. Make frangipane by mixing dry ingredients, then butter and sugar, add egg and zest. Spread jam in pastry, top with frangipane, sprinkle almonds. Bake ~30 min until set.

Name of the Tart: Mini Lemon Tarts
Ingredients:
- Crust: 2 cups flour, 1 cup butter, ½ cup sugar, ½ tsp salt
- Filling: 1 cup sugar, ⅓ cup lemon juice, 3 eggs, 3 tbsp flour, 1 tbsp zest
Instructions:
Preheat oven to 350°F. Prepare crust and press into mini tart pans. Whisk filling, pour into crusts, bake 25–30 min until set. Cool before serving.

Name of the Tart: Sweet Tart Crust (Pâte Sucrée)
Ingredients:
- 1½ cups flour, ½ cup icing sugar, ½ cup almond meal, pinch salt, 1 cup butter, 1 egg
Instructions:
Mix dry ingredients, rub in butter, add egg to form dough. Chill, roll out, and prebake for sweet tarts.

Name of the Tart: Strawberry Tart
Ingredients:
- Pastry: 200g flour, 100g butter, 60g icing sugar, 1 egg
- Crème pâtissière: milk, vanilla, sugar, egg yolks, cornflour, butter
- 750g strawberries, apricot jam
Instructions:
Make pastry, chill, blind bake at 180°C. Prepare custard, cool. Spread jam, fill with custard, top with strawberries, glaze with jam.

Name of the Tart: Fruit Tart with Pastry Cream
Ingredients:
- Shortcrust pastry, apricot jam, vanilla pastry cream, assorted fresh fruit
Instructions:
Blind bake crust at 400°F. Cool, fill with pastry cream, arrange fruit, brush with apricot glaze.
