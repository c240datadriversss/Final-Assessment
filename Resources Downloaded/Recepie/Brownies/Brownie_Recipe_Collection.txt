RECIPE 1: CLASSIC FUDGY COCOA BROWNIES
==================================================

Servings: ~16 brownies
Prep Time: 10–15 minutes
Cook Time: 20–25 minutes
Total Time: 30–40 minutes

INGREDIENTS

Dry Ingredients:
- 2 cups (400 g) granulated sugar
- 1 1/4 cups (155 g) all-purpose flour
- 3/4 cup (75 g) unsweetened cocoa powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

Wet Ingredients:
- 1 cup (225 g) unsalted butter, melted
- 4 large eggs
- 1 tablespoon vanilla extract

Optional Add-ins:
- 1 cup chocolate chips
- 1/2 cup chopped nuts

INSTRUCTIONS

1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Line a 9×13-inch pan.
2. Whisk all dry ingredients together.
3. Stir in melted butter until smooth.
4. Add eggs one at a time, mixing well.
5. Mix in vanilla.
6. Fold in add-ins if using.
7. Bake 20–25 minutes until moist crumbs remain.
8. Cool fully before slicing.

RECIPE 2: CHEWY CRACKLY-TOP BROWNIES
==================================================

Servings: ~16 brownies
Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 25–30 minutes

INGREDIENTS

Dry Ingredients:
- 1 cup (200 g) granulated sugar
- 1/2 cup (100 g) light brown sugar
- 1 cup (125 g) all-purpose flour
- 2/3 cup (65 g) cocoa powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

Wet Ingredients:
- 3/4 cup (170 g) unsalted butter, melted
- 3 large eggs
- 1 tablespoon vanilla extract

Optional Add-ins:
- 1 cup chopped dark chocolate

INSTRUCTIONS

1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Prepare pan.
2. Whisk sugars and eggs vigorously until pale and thick.
3. Whisk in melted butter and vanilla.
4. Fold in dry ingredients gently.
5. Bake 25–30 minutes for chewy texture.
6. Cool completely.

RECIPE 3: ONE-BOWL EASY BROWNIES
==================================================

Servings: ~16 brownies
Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 22–28 minutes

INGREDIENTS

Dry Ingredients:
- 1 3/4 cups (350 g) sugar
- 1 cup (125 g) all-purpose flour
- 1/2 cup (50 g) cocoa powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

Wet Ingredients:
- 1/2 cup (115 g) butter, melted
- 1/2 cup vegetable oil
- 3 large eggs
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Optional Add-ins:
- Chocolate chunks

INSTRUCTIONS

1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
2. Mix all ingredients in one bowl until smooth.
3. Pour into lined pan.
4. Bake 22–28 minutes.
5. Cool before cutting.

RECIPE 4: DEEP CHOCOLATE BUTTER BROWNIES
==================================================

Servings: ~16 brownies
Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 25–30 minutes

INGREDIENTS

Dry Ingredients:
- 1 1/2 cups (300 g) sugar
- 1 cup (125 g) flour
- 1/2 cup (50 g) cocoa powder
- 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

Wet Ingredients:
- 1 cup (225 g) browned butter
- 3 large eggs
- 1 tablespoon vanilla extract

Optional Add-ins:
- Espresso powder
- Chocolate chips

INSTRUCTIONS

1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
2. Brown butter and let cool slightly.
3. Mix sugar and butter until glossy.
4. Add eggs and vanilla.
5. Fold in dry ingredients.
6. Bake 25–30 minutes.

RECIPE 5: ULTRA FUDGY COCOA BROWNIES
==================================================

Servings: ~9–12 brownies
Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 18–22 minutes

INGREDIENTS

Dry Ingredients:
- 1 cup (200 g) sugar
- 3/4 cup (95 g) flour
- 1/2 cup (50 g) cocoa powder
- 1/4 teaspoon salt

Wet Ingredients:
- 1/2 cup (115 g) butter, melted
- 2 large eggs
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

Optional Add-ins:
- Swirl of melted chocolate

INSTRUCTIONS

1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
2. Mix sugar, cocoa, and butter until smooth.
3. Add eggs and vanilla.
4. Fold in flour and salt.
5. Bake 18–22 minutes.
6. Cool fully for maximum fudginess.
