Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours. For urgent matters, please use the chat widget on our website.

Can I use substitutions from your guide in my own recipe blog?

Yes! Please feel free to share and adapt our substitutions. We just ask that you link back to our site and give credit to Team Data Driversss. We're all about making baking more accessible!

Is the chatbot available 24/7?

Yes! The chatbot is always available on our website.

Do you collect personal information?

We respect your privacy. This contact form and the chatbot do not send personal data to external servers. Your information stays with you.

Can I suggest a substitution that's not in your guide?

Absolutely! We're always looking to expand our database. Send us your suggestion with details about how you've used it successfully.

How can I report a mistake in one of the substitutions?

Please let us know immediately through this contact form! Accuracy is our priority, and we appreciate your help in keeping our information current.

Thank You for Your Interest!

Whether you have a question, suggestion, or just want to say hello, we appreciate hearing from you. You're part of our community of dessert lovers!
