Our Mission

We believe that baking should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their ingredient availability or dietary restrictions. Our mission is to make dessert baking easier, faster, and more inclusive by providing instant, reliable ingredient substitution recommendations.

The Dessert Substitution Chatbot was created to solve a real problem: the frustration of discovering you're missing a key ingredient right when you're ready to bake. Instead of abandoning your recipe or spending time researching, you can now chat with our intelligent assistant and get instant solutions.

Why We Built This

The Problem We Saw

Everyone has experienced that sinking feeling: you've found the perfect recipe, gathered most of your ingredients, and then realized you're missing something crucial. Maybe you're out of eggs, or you're making a dessert for a vegan friend and need dairy-free alternatives. The traditional solution? Google frantically through multiple websites or guess-and-check.

Our Solution

We decided to create something better. By combining culinary knowledge with modern technology, we built a chatbot that understands your substitution needs and provides instant, reliable recommendations. No more hunting through recipe blogs or worrying whether a substitution will work. Just ask, and get answers.

How We Did It

Our team spent weeks researching baking science, ingredient chemistry, and culinary best practices. We compiled comprehensive substitution data, tested recommendations, and created an intelligent chatbot that can understand natural language queries and provide contextual answers. The result is a tool that feels like having a pastry chef in your pocket.
